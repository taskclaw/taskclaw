# S005: Pod chat has board context

## Preconditions
- Pod has at least 2 boards with different names
- Pod chat is accessible

## Steps
1. Open the pod's chat
2. Type: "what boards do I have?"
3. Wait for AI response

## Expected Results
- AI lists the actual board names in the pod
- AI does NOT say it has no context or ask what boards exist
- Pod sidebar shows board list matching what AI described

## Viewports
- Desktop: 1280x720
